Small Language Model

Lille, a Compact and Powerful AI

Lille 130M is a 130-million-parameter language model designed as part of a fully open-source deep learning stack, offering strong performance in a compact size.

Available in two variants: Lille-130M-Base, pretrained on 4.27 billion tokens from the FineWeb-Edu dataset for general knowledge, and Lille-130M-Instruct, fine-tuned on the Kyoto-Corpus for instruction-based tasks like chat and command execution.

Powered by a modern Transformer architecture with Grouped-Query Attention, RoPE, and RMSNorm, Lille is optimized for efficiency and performance, making it ideal for research and deployment on modest hardware.

SophiaG Triton

High-Performance LLM Optimizer

Performance Benchmark (ms/step) — Lower is better

SophiaG Triton is my unofficial adaptation of the second-order optimizer by Liu et al., supercharged with Triton for superior GPU performance.

This project focuses on leveraging custom Triton kernels to accelerate computations, achieving up to a 3x speedup over the original implementation and outperforming standard optimizers like AdamW in mixed precision.
