How to make school easy

Bard AI is a large language model developed by Google AI. It can generate texts, translate languages, write different types of creative content and answer questions in an informative way. Bard AI can be used in schools to help students with the following tasks:

  • Research: Bard AI can help students research a topic by finding and summarising relevant information from the Internet.

  • Understanding: Bard AI can help students understand complex concepts by explaining them in simple language.

  • Creativity: Bard AI can help students develop creative ideas by making suggestions and giving feedback.

The use of YouTube

YouTube is a video portal where users can upload and watch videos on a variety of topics. YouTube can be used in school to help students with the following tasks:

  • Learning: YouTube offers a wide range of educational videos on various subjects and topics. These videos can help students to learn new content and prepare for exams.

  • Communication: YouTube can be used to communicate and collaborate with other students and teachers.

  • Self-presentation: YouTube can be used to present your own projects and work.

Bard AI and YouTube are two powerful tools that can facilitate and enhance learning in school. They can be used by students and teachers to conduct research, understand complex concepts, be creative and connect with others.
