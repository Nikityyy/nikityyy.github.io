Musify

What devices can use Musify?

It only works on Desktop.

Why is it only showing a 30-second preview?

You have to log in at Spotify to play the full songs for free with no ads.

How do I use it?

You enter the link of the Song, Artist, Podcast, or Playlist, and the website automatically retrieves the embedded link. The embedded links do not have any ads, but unfortunately, they only work correctly on Desktop devices.

Can Musify play private playlists?

Of course. Since you log in at Spotify it uses your account for handling the music.
